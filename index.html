The Cost of Freedom - Book Sprint

Call for participation

Nov 2nd to 6th - Marseilles, France

The Cost of Freedom is a book that will be written in Marseilles from November 2nd to 6th. We are looking for essayists, illustrators, coders, artists, designers, researchers, hackers, writers and members of all free cultures to join the effort and take part in this Book Sprint.

All for free, free for all. Just download, click and go. With Internet running on free softwares, music and news available all around, free access have become the new normal of our digital lives.

Yet, free culture comes at a price. Who is paying for it ? What are the costs ? What can we learn from years of free cultures activism, failures and success ?

During 5 days, a group of 10-12 people will gather in Marseilles to discuss how cultures are built and what are the real costs attached to them. We will work together to see further than the fog of our news feeds and produce some sense by using the Book Sprint methodology created by Adam Hyde.

The Cost of Freedom will be published on Nov 6 and freely available online under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 license.

info & rsvp : contact@costoffreedom.cc
